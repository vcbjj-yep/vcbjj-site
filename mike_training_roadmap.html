Not a grip battle. Not a strategy. One connection — pull it, assess what it gives you, make the next one. Session #32: reset after each phase of the game, map funnels, do not obsess over grip fighting.

This is the check. Any time you feel stuck in a position, ask: am I pulling or pushing? If you are pushing, you have handed the initiative to your partner. Find the pull.

Your best rolls have come when you stopped checking whether the moment was decisive enough. Session #31: the BJJ ruleset tolerates exploration, error, and accumulation. Give yourself the full game to work with.
