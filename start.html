Participation Agreement — read before signing

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is a contact sport. Training involves physical engagement with other participants and carries inherent risks — including bruising, sprains, strains, and other injuries — that cannot be fully eliminated by instruction, supervision, or equipment.

By participating in any VCBJJ session (private training, group class, open mat, or workshop), you voluntarily accept these risks and agree to the following:

1. Inherent Risk. You acknowledge that BJJ training carries inherent risks of injury and that no level of coaching or supervision can eliminate this risk entirely.

2. Contact Injuries. You accept that injuries may occur through contact with training partners during drills, partner exercises, or sparring (rolling), and you release VCBJJ and its coach from liability for such injuries unless caused by gross negligence.

3. Medical Conditions. You confirm that you are physically capable of participating in martial arts training. You agree to inform your coach of any pre-existing medical conditions, injuries, or health concerns before each session. You release VCBJJ from liability for any aggravation of undisclosed pre-existing conditions.

4. Photo & Video Consent. Sessions may be photographed or filmed for coaching review or VCBJJ social media. You consent to this unless you separately notify your coach in writing. You may withdraw consent at any time.

5. Voluntary Participation. You sign this agreement of your own free will as a release of liability.
