Your First Step

Get Started

Five steps from here to the mat. No gatekeeping — just a clear process.

1
Read the Info Page
Make sure VCBJJ is the right fit for you. We’re upfront about what we are and what we’re not. Read it here →
2
Register Your Interest
Fill out the contact form so we know you’re coming. We’ll confirm your details and answer any questions. Contact form →
3
Sign the Participation Agreement
BJJ is a contact sport. Before your first session, you’ll sign a participation agreement acknowledging the inherent risks. This is done digitally through the member portal.
4
Make Your First Payment
Transfer via bank or scan the QR code below. Once done, send your payment receipt via WhatsApp for confirmation.
WhatsApp is used only for payment receipt confirmation. Please do not call — all enquiries go through the contact form.
5
Book Your First Session
Once your payment is confirmed, you’ll get access to the member portal where you can book group classes or private training sessions. Member portal →
